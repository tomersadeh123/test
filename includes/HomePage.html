About Us

Smart Shared Schedule Management Application

We Are SmartOrganizer

This application for managing shared schedules will assist users in organizing and managing all aspects of their calendars efficiently and comprehensively. The application will integrate artificial intelligence and data analysis to understand each user's patterns and preferences. It will synchronize all meetings, appointments, and tasks across various time frames—daily, weekly, and monthly.

The uniqueness of the application lies in its ability to plan the user's day optimally, taking into account their priorities, emotional state, and workload. It will feature user-friendly interfaces and allow for personalization, enabling each user to manage their time in the best possible way for them.

Initially, the system will pull relevant information from the user's Google Calendar. Based on this information, the application will offer users the best options for meetings with different groups, according to the available times of all involved users. Once all group members select the suitable times, the system will display and schedule the meeting in the calendars of those users.

Additionally, the application will analyze the user's free times and suggest ways to fill them. For example, if the system detects that the user hasn't exercised on a particular day and has a certain time slot available, it will suggest that they go for a workout during that time.
